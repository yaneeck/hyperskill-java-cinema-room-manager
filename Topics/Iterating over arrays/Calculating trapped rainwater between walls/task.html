Calculating trapped rainwater between walls

Hey there! This problem might be a bit unpredictable, but give it a go and let us know how you do!
You are given an array of non-negative integers representing the heights of walls, and the width between every two consecutive walls is 1 unit. Rain can be trapped in the gaps between the walls, but not on top of the walls. Calculate the total volume of water that can be trapped between the walls.
Sample Input:
3
4
2
0
2

Sample Output:
4


Sample Input:
2
3
4
5
2

Sample Output:
0
